# Hood Filter Sizing Guide

Source: https://www.hoodfilters.com/flyers/Hood_Filter_Sizing_guide.pdf

**Install baffles vertically (top to bottom)** so grease runs down to the trough and into the grease tray.

**Ordering convention:** vertical (top to bottom) dimension first, horizontal (left to right) second.

**Tip:** Measure the opening, round up to the nearest ½", then find the matching trade size below.

## Trade Size vs. Actual Dimensions

| Trade Size (H × W) | Actual Dimensions (H × W × D) |
|--------------------|-------------------------------|
| 10" × 16"          | 9-⅝" × 15-⅝" × 1-⅞"         |
| 10" × 20"          | 9-⅝" × 19-⅝" × 1-⅞"         |
| 12" × 16"          | 11-⅝" × 15-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |
| 12" × 20"          | 11-⅝" × 19-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |
| 12" × 24"          | 11-⅝" × 23-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |
| 16" × 16"          | 15-⅝" × 15-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |
| 16" × 20"          | 15-⅝" × 19-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |
| 16" × 25"          | 15-⅝" × 24-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |
| 20" × 10"          | 19-⅝" × 9-⅝" × 1-⅞"         |
| 20" × 16"          | 19-⅝" × 15-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |
| 20" × 20"          | 19-⅝" × 19-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |
| 20" × 25"          | 19-⅝" × 24-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |
| 25" × 16"          | 24-⅝" × 15-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |
| 25" × 20"          | 24-⅝" × 19-⅝" × 1-⅞"        |

If measurements don't match a standard size, rounding up won't apply — contact the hood manufacturer.
