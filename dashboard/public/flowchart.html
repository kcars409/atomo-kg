Status Definitions

Active
Working, Contacted, Inspection stages, Proposal stages — in the review queue.
Closed Won
Signed. Ops handoff initiated. Scheduling pending or confirmed.
Cold
Door not shut. No response, not ready, went elsewhere. Re-engage someday.
Closed Lost
Permanently off the list. Bad fit, business closed, or actively unwanted.
⛔ DNC Flag
do_not_contact: true. Any status. Always shows warning. Requires confirmation for any outreach.
